WHY KAUSHAL?

Extracurricular activities and skills plays a crucial role in holistic development. However, these programs are often the first to be cut in schools facing budget constraints, depriving students of essential opportunities for growth and expression.

Many families cannot afford to pay for skill based development, leaving their children without the chance to explore and develop their talents and interests outside the standard curriculum.

ABOUT US

'Kaushal Up' is established with the vision of empowering individuals through skill-based learning and education. We recognised the gap in accessible education as well as skill development opportunities for underprivileged communities and set out to bridge this gap for the welfare of the society.

Our journey began when we noticed that many talented individuals lacked the resources and opportunities to gain education and develop their skills. Determined to make a difference, we have started offering free sessions in our community.

As we look ahead, 'Kaushal Up' aims to continue expanding its reach and impact even more lives.

HOW WE HELP

'Kaushal Up' offers a variety of educational and skill-based learning programs designed to empower individuals through education and creativity. Our programs are free of charge and accessible to those who cannot afford conventional training.
